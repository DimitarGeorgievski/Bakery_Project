Ingredients & Methods

To create a delectable loaf of bread, you will need high-quality bread flour,salt, active dry yeast,lukewarm water. Begin by combining the flour and salt in a large bowl. In a separate container, dissolve the yeast in the lukewarm water. Make a well in the flour mixture and pour in the yeast mixture. Gradually incorporate the flour into the liquid, kneading until a smooth dough forms. Cover the bowl with a damp cloth and allow the dough to rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size. Preheat the oven to 220Â°C. Shape the dough, place it in a greased loaf tin, and let it rise for an additional 30 minutes. Finally, bake for 25-30 minutes until the bread achieves a delightful golden brown hue.
